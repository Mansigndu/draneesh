About the Service

Gentle, Evidence-Based Support Through Menopause

Menopause can bring physical, emotional, and hormonal changes that affect day-to-day life. With the right support, women can feel more balanced and comfortable during this transition.

Dr. Aneesh Sabnis provides individualized care for symptoms such as hot flashes, mood changes, sleep disruption, and bone health concerns.

Common Symptoms

  • Hot flashes and night sweats
  • Sleep disturbance or fatigue
  • Mood changes and anxiety
  • Vaginal dryness or low libido

Symptoms & Indicators

Hot Flashes

Sudden warmth and sweating can disrupt sleep and daily comfort.

Sleep Problems

Restlessness and night waking may worsen energy and mood.

Mood Changes

Mood swings and irritability can be linked to hormone shifts.

Bone Health Concerns

Hormonal changes can increase the need for bone protection and screening.

Causes & Risk Factors

What Drives Menopausal Symptoms

  • Hormonal shifts and declining estrogen
  • Stress, sleep habits, and lifestyle factors
  • Weight changes and metabolic health
  • Previous medical history and family patterns
Diagnosis

Evidence-Based Assessment

  • Symptom review and medical history
  • Hormonal and blood work if needed
  • Bone health and metabolic evaluation
  • Personalized treatment recommendations

Treatment Options

Symptom Management

Treatment can focus on hot flashes, sleep quality, mood, and vaginal comfort.

Hormone Therapy Review

Hormone replacement therapies can be discussed based on medical suitability and goals.

Lifestyle Guidance

Nutrition, exercise, sleep, and stress strategies are integrated into care.

Benefits of Treatment
  • Better symptom control and comfort
  • Improved sleep, mood, and energy
  • Support for bone and heart health
  • More confidence and quality of life
Treatment Process
  • Initial consultation and symptom review
  • Testing and health assessment
  • Individualized care plan
  • Ongoing monitoring and adjustment
