Professional Biography

Dr. Aneesh Sabnis is a highly respected Consultant Gynaecologist and Obstetrician with over 30+ years of dedicated experience in women's healthcare. Throughout his distinguished career, he has established himself as a pioneer in advanced laparoscopic surgery and comprehensive reproductive medicine.

With a compassionate approach to patient care and an unwavering commitment to excellence, Dr. Sabnis has successfully managed thousands of cases spanning normal deliveries, high-risk pregnancies, complex surgical procedures, and fertility treatments. His expertise extends to minimally invasive techniques that ensure optimal outcomes with minimal patient discomfort.

Beyond his clinical practice, Dr. Sabnis remains actively involved in continuous medical education, staying abreast of the latest advancements in gynecology and obstetrics. He is known for his patient-centric approach, taking time to educate and involve patients in their healthcare decisions.
