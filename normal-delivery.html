Symptoms & Labor Signs

Regular Contractions

Consistent contractions are a sign that labor is progressing naturally.

Water Breaking

Rupture of membranes often marks the onset of active labor.

Lower Back Pressure

Persistent back pressure or cramping can also accompany labor.

Bloody Show

A mucous discharge with blood may indicate labor is approaching.
