About the Service

Specialized Support for High-Risk Pregnancies

A high-risk pregnancy requires proactive care and individualized monitoring. Dr. Aneesh Sabnis provides advanced maternal-fetal support for conditions such as gestational diabetes, hypertension, multiple gestation, placental complications, and prior pregnancy concerns.

Each treatment plan is tailored to ensure both maternal safety and healthy fetal development, while keeping the patient informed and supported throughout the journey.

What to Expect

  • Detailed history review and risk assessment
  • Regular fetal monitoring and growth scans
  • Personalized nutrition, medication, and rest plans
  • Timely intervention if complications arise

Symptoms & Warning Signs

Persistent Swelling

Facial or limb swelling may indicate rising blood pressure or fluid retention.

Severe Headaches

Headaches or visual disturbances can be associated with preeclampsia.

Reduced Fetal Movement

A noticeable drop in fetal activity warrants urgent evaluation.

Abdominal Pain

Pain or tightness may signal complications requiring immediate assessment.

Causes & Risk Factors

Common Conditions We Manage

  • Gestational diabetes and pre-existing diabetes
  • Preeclampsia, hypertension, and placental issues
  • Multiple pregnancies or prior miscarriages
  • Advanced maternal age or thyroid concerns
Diagnosis

Precision Evaluation

  • Routine blood pressure and urine monitoring
  • Ultrasound and fetal growth assessments
  • Blood sugar, thyroid, and infection screenings
  • Detailed review of medical history and previous pregnancies

Treatment Options

Close Monitoring

Regular checkups, blood pressure tracking, and targeted testing to keep mother and baby safe.

Medical Management

Medication, nutrition guidance, and individualized therapy to manage complications effectively.

Delivery Planning

Careful planning for delivery timing and route, including hospital coordination if needed.
