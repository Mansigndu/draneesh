About the Service

Gentle, Confidential Care for Young Women

Adolescent gynaecology focuses on the unique reproductive health needs of teenage girls and young women. These visits can address periods, puberty changes, pain, acne-related concerns, and education around reproductive health.

The care is designed to be supportive, respectful, and age-appropriate while building confidence and awareness.

Common Concerns

  • Irregular or painful periods
  • Puberty-related questions
  • PCOS symptoms and weight changes
  • Reproductive health education and counseling

Symptoms & Indicators

Painful Periods

Severe cramps or heavy bleeding can affect school, activity, and wellbeing.

Delayed Periods

Irregular cycles may require evaluation and guidance.

Acne or Hair Growth

These signs may be linked to hormonal health and deserve attention.

Low Confidence / Anxiety

Reproductive health concerns may affect emotional wellbeing and self-esteem.

Causes & Risk Factors

Common Contributing Factors

  • Hormonal fluctuations during puberty
  • Polycystic ovary syndrome or thyroid issues
  • Stress, diet, and lifestyle influences
  • Underlying structural or reproductive health concerns
Diagnosis

A Safe and Supportive Evaluation

  • Detailed history and symptom review
  • Clinical examination and ultrasound when appropriate
  • Hormonal and blood testing if needed
  • Education and guidance for ongoing care

Treatment Options

Education & Counseling

A sensitive, age-appropriate conversation about menstrual and reproductive health.

Medical Management

Treatment for pain, hormonal issues, and irregular cycles when needed.

Ongoing Support

Follow-up care helps protect wellbeing as the patient grows and develops.

Benefits of Treatment
  • Relief from menstrual pain and irregularity
  • Improved confidence and wellbeing
  • Earlier understanding of health concerns
  • Supportive guidance for future reproductive health
Treatment Process
  • Initial consultation and privacy-centered conversation
  • Clinical assessment and relevant tests
  • Personalized treatment and counseling
  • Regular follow-up as the patient grows
