About the Service

Comprehensive Fertility Evaluation and Treatment

Infertility can be emotionally challenging, but the right evaluation and support can make a meaningful difference. Dr. Aneesh Sabnis offers fertility assessment, hormone evaluation, ovulation support, and guidance for assisted reproductive pathways.

The focus is on understanding the underlying cause and creating a treatment plan that aligns with your goals, health, and values.

Key Services

  • Fertility history and medical evaluation
  • Hormone and ovulation assessment
  • IVF and ICSI support planning
  • Emotional support and counseling guidance

Symptoms & Indicators

Irregular Periods

Cycle irregularity may suggest ovulatory issues or hormonal imbalance.

Recurrent Miscarriage

Repeated pregnancy loss may warrant a detailed fertility work-up.

Endometriosis

This condition can affect fertility and may require treatment planning.

PCOS

Polycystic ovarian syndrome often affects ovulation and conception.

Causes & Risk Factors

Common Factors Behind Infertility

  • Ovulatory dysfunction or hormonal imbalance
  • Endometriosis or uterine conditions
  • Male factor infertility or sperm concerns
  • Age-related changes and prior reproductive history
Diagnosis

A Thorough Clinical Work-Up

  • Medical history and fertility assessment
  • Hormone testing and pelvic ultrasound
  • Partner evaluation when appropriate
  • Customized referral and treatment roadmap
